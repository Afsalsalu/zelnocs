ONLINE TICKET BOOKING

Samatwa Service Centers (SSCs) are part of the Digital India initiative and provide various online booking services for citizens. These centers help people book tickets for travel, government services, and other appointments without needing a personal internet connection or digital literacy. Types of ssc Online Booking Services

SSC ACADEMY

SSC Academy is an initiative of the Common Services Centers (SSC) program under the Government of India. It provides digital literacy, skill development, and professional training programs to empower citizens, especially in rural and semi-urban areas. The academy offers courses in IT, entrepreneurship, financial literacy, cybersecurity, and various vocational skills, helping individuals improve their employability and business opportunities. Through online and offline learning, SSC Academy aims to bridge the digital divide and promote inclusive education. It collaborates with government agencies, private organizations, and educational institutions to deliver high-qualitytraining.
